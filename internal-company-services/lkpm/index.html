LKPM

WHAT IS LKPM?

LKPM, or Laporan Kegiatan Penanaman Modal, is more than a periodic report—it’s a testament to your business’s commitment, progress, and alignment with regulatory standards. It encapsulates your investment activities, ensuring transparency and adherence to the nation’s developmental vision. Our platform is proficient in guiding businesses through the meticulous process of LKPM preparation, submission, and compliance, anchoring your endeavors in trust and regulatory harmony.

WHAT DOCUMENTS ARE REQUIRED

  • Detailed investment activity report.

  • Financial statements, including profit and loss accounts.

  • Documentation showcasing the progress of the investment project.

  • Employment data related to the investment.

  • Proof of adherence to local regulatory standards.

  • Previous LKPM reports (for reference).

  • Any regulatory or government correspondence related to the investment.

  • Data on technology transfers or innovations, if applicable.

  • Environmental compliance documents, if relevant.

  • Other specific documentation based on the nature of the investment or jurisdictional requirements.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

LKPM

What is the purpose of the LKPM?

LKPM is a periodic report submitted by investors to detail their investment activities, ensuring that they align with the nation's regulatory and developmental goals.

How often do I need to submit LKPM?

Typically, LKPM needs to be submitted semi-annually, though there might be variations based on specific regulations or the nature of the investment.

What happens if I fail to submit LKPM on time?

Delay or failure to submit can lead to penalties, ranging from fines to potential revocation of investment privileges. It’s essential to adhere to deadlines.

Can LKPM be submitted online?

Many jurisdictions have introduced online portals for the submission of LKPM, simplifying the process and increasing efficiency. It's advisable to check local regulations.

Do all businesses need to submit LKPM?

Generally, businesses with a certain threshold of investment or those availing specific incentives are required to submit LKPM. However, it's crucial to consult local regulations to determine applicability.
