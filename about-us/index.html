EMPOWERING INTERNATIONALS IN BALI

As a foreigner, relocating, establishing, and investing in Indonesia has a great deal of appeal; an unparalleled lifestyle, never ending sunsets and surf breaks, and the opportunity to tap into a rapidly growing economy with a young and dynamic population. However, it is important to understand and comply with the local laws and regulations, as well as the cultural norms and business practices adopted in Bali. Trying to independently navigate the intricacies of the Bali commercial, cultural and legal system can be a daunting task. Working with an experienced firm, specializing in delivering legal and business services to expats, means you can confidently navigate the unique challenges that foreigners face in Bali, whether as a resident, retiree, business, or investor.

We specialise in working with foreigners in Indonesia and offer a wide range of services commonly faced by individuals seeking to relocate, invest and operate businesses in Bali to provide a one stop solution for foreigners. These include strategic planning, risk management, translation and employer of record services, as well as expert advice and tailored, practical solutions to help you navigate the complex Bali legal and regulatory landscape.

We pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and personalized attention at a cost which is reflective of work output. We are also committed to building long-term relationships based on trust and transparency, to help you to achieve your immediate and longer term island dream.

OUR TEAM

Amie, an accomplished Australian lawyer with 12 years at top law firms, founded Crichton & Co Legal in 2018. With Indonesian roots, she’s deeply connected to Bali and splits her time between there and Sydney with her two sons. Passionate about affordable premium legal services, she established BXS in Bali to address this. Committed to environmental efforts and uplifting the local community, Amie fosters a collaborative, innovative corporate culture. In her downtime, she enjoys surfing in Canggu, biking, and appreciating Bali’s sunsets.

Felix Winata, an Indonesian lawyer from Gadjah Mada University, joined Amie’s team in 2022, overseeing BXS’ local operations and paralegal team. Previously, he worked at Bali Legal Aid Foundation and DSP Law Firm in Jakarta, specializing in corporate and litigation matters. Beyond BXS, Felix is a sports fan, a skilled chess player, and enjoys forging new friendships.

Muhammad Ananda Fakhri is a seasoned Digital Marketing Specialist with a Master’s from Universitas Sumatera Utara (USU). With over seven years in the field, he’s adept at crafting impactful online campaigns, blending academic insights with practical expertise. His knowledge ensures brands stay industry-leading. Beyond his professional achievements, Ananda is dedicated to personal growth and stays updated with the latest digital trends.
